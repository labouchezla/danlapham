About

I’ve spent the last decade leading organic performance and search strategy for complex organisations, most recently as Executive Business Director at OMD UK.

My work sits beyond traditional SEO. I focus on how discovery is changing across AI-driven search, zero-click environments and blurred channel boundaries, building systems that enable sustainable growth rather than short-term optimisation.

I’m particularly interested in building teams that are ready for the next era of search. I work with organisations to align SEO, PPC, content and UX around shared signals, so that discovery becomes efficent and scalable.
