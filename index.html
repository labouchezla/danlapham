About

I’ve spent the last decade leading organic performance and search strategy for complex organisations, most recently as Executive Business Director at OMD UK.

My work sits beyond traditional SEO. I focus on how discovery is changing across AI-driven search, platforms, zero-click environments and blurred channel boundaries — and what that means for strategy, operating models and commercial decision-making.

I’m particularly interested in the moments where complexity needs to be reduced to clarity: setting direction, making trade-offs, and building systems that enable sustainable growth rather than short-term optimisation.

What I work on

Search & discovery in transition

  • Interpreting shifts across AI Overviews, LLMs, zero-click and social discovery.
  • Reframing success from “rankings” to visibility, influence and conversion journeys.
  • Turning change into clear priorities leaders can fund and execute.

Strategy, prioritisation & trade-offs

  • Pressure-testing assumptions and making constraints explicit.
  • Building roadmaps linked to commercial outcomes (not activity).
  • Creating decision clarity across brand vs non-brand and platform vs platform.

Leadership & operating models

  • Federated and integrated delivery models for complex organisations.
  • Craft standards, governance and rituals that sustain quality at scale.
  • Measurement systems that make performance legible to senior stakeholders.
